  2. Export your token: export DOCS_MCP_TOKEN=tok_…

Flash Protocol

The bootloader speaks a small framed protocol over UART at 115200 baud. Each frame is acknowledged before the next is sent.

Always erase the sector before a write, or the CRC check fails.
0xAA | LEN(2) | CMD | PAYLOAD | CRC16
ACK  = 0x79
NACK = 0x1F
